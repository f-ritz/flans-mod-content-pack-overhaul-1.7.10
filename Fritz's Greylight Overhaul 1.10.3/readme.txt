Permissions for use:
--------------------
You are allowed to use my pack for your personal, or private/public use in a server. Distribution of my pack must be complete, with all files.

The only modifications that can be made to the pack, are item names and the stats of the guns. You can also add/make additional skins for the guns to your pleasure.
- Attachments and item parts should not be changed, preferably because they are in fixed positions on each gun, and some attachments are specialised for a specific gun. 
Removing weapons and adding them to another pack is not allowed either.

- Items/weapons can be disabled depending on the limitations you require for your server.

- Crediting is needed. You must in some way, credit my work.

- This pack is written with the full intention of plagarism prevention. I am very slack on monetisation, I will only be alert if you attempt direct plagarism.
